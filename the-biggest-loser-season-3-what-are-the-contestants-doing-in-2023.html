The Biggest Loser Season 3: What Are The Contestants Doing In 2023?

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Since its debut in 2004, the reality TV programme “The Biggest Loser” has grown in popularity. During the course of a few weeks, overweight contestants battle to reduce weight. The candidates are divided into two teams, each of which is led by a personal trainer. When just two contestants are left, the competition is over, and the victor is picked based on how much weight they shed.

The third season, which debuted in September 2009, was presented by Caroline Rhea. The audience took to it right away, and it inspired millions to take control of their health and wellbeing. One of the most popular weight loss programmes in the world is now this show. It makes sense for viewers of season 3 of “The Biggest Loser” to be curious about the whereabouts of the contestants. Thankfully, we have all the solutions.

Where is Erik Chopin Now?

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Erik Chopin (@erikchopin)


Erik Chopin struggled to lose weight and keep it off before he started the show. While he was the show’s biggest competitor at the time, he shed an amazing 214 pounds (97 kg), shattering all prior records. He retained that record until season 8 in 2009. Chopin needed plastic surgery to have extra skin removed after his victory. He started speaking at wellness expos, fitness clubs, and schools as a motivational speaker. He has had multiple television appearances, including ones on “The Oprah Winfrey Show” and “Larry King Live.”

Chopin was also mentioned in periodicals like People and The New York Times. He and his family made an appearance in a Discovery Health Channel documentary in 2010 to talk about his weight increase since the show and its effects on his life. He continues to tour the nation as a motivational speaker and has subsequently earned his certification as a life coach. Chopin works at Emma’s Delicatessen in North Babylon, which is owned by his family, and has an accounting degree from Hofstra University. He has three kids and is married.

Where is Kai Hibbard Now?

A post shared by Kai Hibbard (@kaihibbard)


Kai Hibbard enlisted in the Alaska Army National Guard in August 2011, but due to injuries received while serving on active duty, he was released in 2014. She continues to promote body acceptance and has delivered speeches at gatherings and conferences, such as the East Coast Body Positive Fitness Alliance Conference in 2016 and the national conference of the Binge Eating Disorder Association in 2015.

In a 2015 piece for XoJane, Hibbard wrote about her experiences with weight loss reality TV. ‘Losing It: A Fictional Reimagining of My Time on Weight Loss Reality TV,’ a fictitious description of her time on the programme, was released in 2018 by the author. She has additionally been on CNN HLN as a body acceptance activist.

Where is Mark Wylie Now?

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Mark J. Wylie (@markjwylie)

For 485 straight days, Mark Wylie, a disability rights activist and past participant on “The Biggest Loser,” has been going on daily walks across his Miami Beach neighbourhood. As a gay, unmarried, and Black guy during the COVID-19 lockdowns, he developed this regimen to help him deal with his loneliness and worry.

The “Come Walk With Me” initiative, which Wylie started out of his daily walks, exhorts others to prioritise their physical and emotional health by going for daily walks. He uses his platform to encourage others to walk because he believes it is an easy yet effective technique to improve overall health and well-being. Wylie has a beneficial impact on both his community and the world at large through his advocacy efforts with Best Buddies and “Come Walk With Me.”

Where is Heather Hansen Now?


Heather Hansen was a 294-pound homemaker from Bountiful, Utah. She put a lot of effort into her weight loss throughout her time on the show, reducing to 173 pounds. Heather continued to put emphasis on her health and fitness after her experience on “The Biggest Loser,” becoming a Certified Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor. She also became an agent with American Family Insurance, and because to her change and weight loss, she is now able to assist her clients in achieving their wellness and health goals. Many people find inspiration in Heather, and she keeps using her platform to motivate others to take charge of their health and wellbeing. She continues to be an active member of the fitness and health community, and many media sites have covered her journey.

Where is Jaron Tate Now?

In Hot Springs, Arkansas, Jaron Tate went back to working as a postal worker after making an appearance on “The Biggest Loser” in 2007. Yet it’s not known if he kept working towards a profession in weight loss or fitness following the show. While Jaron lives a solitary life away from social media, there have been no new updates regarding his activities or whereabouts.

Where is Adrian Kortesmaki Now?

She was motivated to adopt a healthy lifestyle and set new objectives by Adrian Kortesmaki’s experience on “The Biggest Loser.” She developed a passion for running and ran a full marathon in addition to her goal of competing in the American Birkebeiner cross-country ski marathon. Following the show, Kortesmaki remained committed to improving her physical condition, and she has now emerged as a role model for anyone seeking to make positive changes in their life.

In addition, Kortesmaki has participated in charity activities to promote childhood obesity awareness and raise money for cancer research. The couple has four kids together, and the five of them are content as a family. The reality television personality has kept up her love of skiing and has taken part in a number of competitions, including the American Birkebeiner.

Where is Marty Wolff Now?


Marty Wolff is a native of Omaha, Nebraska, and the president of Square One Fitness Club in addition to being a professional speaker. He is referred to as the “Chubby Whisperer,” owns the website ChubbyWhisperer.com, speaks publicly, and promotes wellness and health. Former “The Biggest Loser” contestants Marty Wolff and Amy Hildreth wed in a tiny ceremony in Charleston, South Carolina, in front of just 20 people. The couple, who were romantically involved on the show, married to make their union official. They are a loving and content family with their two children.

Where is Bobby Moore Now?

Chicago firefighter Bobby Moore was a well-liked contestant on the show. He doesn’t have any active public accounts on social media, though, and has kept a low profile. He has generally avoided the spotlight since his time on the show, so it’s unknown what he has been doing. Moore’s motivational tale of weight loss and perseverance on the show nonetheless continues to motivate many. He probably keeps helping his community and having a positive influence on others around him as a firefighter.
