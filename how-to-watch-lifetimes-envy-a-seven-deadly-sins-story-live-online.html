How To Watch Lifetimes Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story Live Online

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Last week, the first film in Lifetime’s Seven Deadly Sins anthology series, Lust, premiered on the network. Tonight, we’re all about to be various shades of green as Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story makes its Lifetime debut.

Based on Victoria Christopher Murray’s bestselling book of the same name, Envy follows the story of Gabrielle Flores (Rose Rollins), a woman with a seemingly perfect life who discovers she has a half-sister, Keisha (Serayah). What begins as a celebration soon turns dark as Keisha, who grew up with nothing, wants Gabrielle’s life, and she’ll do whatever it takes to get it.

Here’s how to watch Lifetime’s Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story live online.

WHEN IS ENVY: A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY ON LIFETIME?

Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story premieres Saturday, April 17 on Lifetime.

WHAT TIME DOES ENVY: A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY AIR ON LIFETIME?

The film airs from 8:00-10:03 p.m. ET on Lifetime. Encore presentations will air at 12:01 a.m. ET and 6:00 p.m. ET on Sunday, April 18.

HOW TO WATCH ENVY A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY LIVE ON LIFETIME:

If you have a valid cable login, you can watch Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story live on the Lifetime website or Lifetime app (available on iTunes, Roku, and more). The film will be available for next-day streaming on the Lifetime website.

You can also watch Lifetime movies anytime with a subscription to the Lifetime Movie Club. Available for $3.99/month or $39.99/year, Lifetime offers a seven-day free trial for eligible subscribers.

HOW TO WATCH ENVY A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY WITHOUT CABLE:

You can watch Envy A Seven Deadly Sins Story live with an active subscription to an over-the-top streaming service that offers Lifetime. Thankfully, you have a number of options, including fuboTV, Philo, Hulu + Live TV, and Sling TV.

Both fuboTV and Philo offer free trials for eligible subscribers.

WILL ENVY A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY BE ON HULU?

Nope. Unfortunately, the film won’t be available for next-day streaming with a traditional Hulu account. You can, however, stream Envy: A Seven Deadly Sins Story live or on demand with an active subscription to Hulu + Live TV.

WILL ENVY: A SEVEN DEADLY SINS STORY BE ON NETFLIX?

Not in the near future. While it’s possible the film eventually finds its way onto Netflix, the movie won’t be available on the streaming service anytime soon.
