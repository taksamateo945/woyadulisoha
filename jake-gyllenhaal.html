Jake Gyllenhaal Biography

Jake Gyllenhaal is best known as Actor, Producer who has an estimated Net Worth of $80 Million. Actor who had his breakthrough role in October Sky and won a BAFTA Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Brokeback Mountain. He also had memorable roles in The Day After Tomorrow, Zodiac, Nightcrawler, and Love and Other Drugs.  He was born on December 19,1980, Los Angeles, CA. He starred in the cult classic film Donnie Darko, for which he won the 2003 Chlotrudis Award for Best Actor.  He worked as a lifeguard and busboy as a teenager. In 1991, he debuted as Danny Robbins in City Slickers.  He is the godfather of Heath Ledger's daughter, Matilda.   He is the brother of actress Maggie Gyllenhaal. His parents worked in Hollywood as directors and screenwriters.  His zodiac sign is Sagittarius
