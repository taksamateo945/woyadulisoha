Rapper Sheff G Posts Prison Pic

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Rapper Sheff G posted a pic from prison wishing his supporters a happy new year.

“Happy Blue Years ? I AINT YA REGULAR G MEMBER EVERYBODY KNOW IM SANCTION ? #FREEME #AYOOOCIIID #TENTOES” he captioned the shirtless pic.

Sheff G was sentenced to two years in prison after pleading guilty in connection with a traffic stop arrest earlier this year.

Sheff pleaded guilty to criminal possession of a weapon in the second degree after he was arrested during a traffic stop, allegedly being driving recklessly and without a license plate. After police searched his car, they found a .45 caliber Glock. “NYPD SUCK MY D*CK,” Sheff posted on Instagram Stories.

In July he was arrested for second-degree felony gun possession in New York. Sheff G is currently serving his time at the Robert N. Davoren Center at Rikers Island.

In September he released “Drum Dummy” from behind bars.

“Yeah they left me, the same ones, they turnin’ back now/Love to see my family happy, sh*t we blessed now/Yeah we blessed now, remember we was stressed out/Hundred K just for this necklace, this bitch two-toned, n*gga/She just pull up, get to neckin’, sh*t we too grown for this/Took some losses, I’m still winnin’, I’m a keep on winning,” he rapped.
