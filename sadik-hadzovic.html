Who is Sadik Hadzovic:

Sadik Hadzovic is a famous Bodybuilder. He was born on June 8, 1987 and his birthplace is Bosnia And Herzegovina. Sadik is also well known as, IFBB professional classic physique competitor and fitness model who became known for winning the first ever Arnold Sports Festival Pro Men’s Physique contest in 2015. He has also been featured in publications such as Muscle & Fitness, Fitness RX, and more.

Sadik is originated from Bosnia And Herzegovina. He participated in the 2015 IFBB Arnold Sports Festival, which is named after bodybuilder Arnold Schwarzenegger.
