RAYE Shares Powerful New Music Video For 'Escapism.' &amp; Announces 2023 World Tour

Publish date: 2024-05-20

As she gears up for the release of her highly anticipated debut album ‘My 21st Century Blues’ early next year, RAYE drops the music video for her latest single ‘Escapism.’

Directed by the singer, along with writer and filmmaker Otis Dominique, the powerful clip brings the tracks painful and jarring lyrics to life, as RAYE recounts the dark details of a brutal break-up that left her spiralling into a hedonistic, and self-destructive way of life as a way to mask the inner torment eating away at her soul having been cast aside by a former lover.

Watch the official music video for ‘Escapism.’ below:

In celebration of the release of her album, RAYE will hit the road next year as part of the M21CB Tour – a global trek around the world that will see the London native play shows in the UK, Europe Canada, and America.

Scheduled to kick off in the Netherlands on February 25th, before wrapping up in New York on March 21st, tickets for all shows go on sale Friday, November 18th at 10am local time.

To get priority pre-sale access to the UK tour dates, fans can pre-order ‘My 21st Century Blues’ in any format from the RAYE store (rayeofficial.com) before 23:00 GMT Tuesday November 15th to receive an exclusive ticket pre-sale code. The UK pre-sale begins 10:00AM GMT Wednesday November 16th. Top fans across the UK, Europe and the US who follow RAYE on Spotify can also look out for an email on Thursday November 16th for special tour access.
