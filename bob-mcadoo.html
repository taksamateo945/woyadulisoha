Who is Bob McAdoo:

Bob McAdoo is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on September 25, 1951 and his birthplace is Greensboro, NC. Bob is also well known as, NBA Hall of Fame center/forward who led the league in scoring for three consecutive seasons from 1974 to 1976 as a member of the Buffalo Braves. He then went on to win two NBA Championships as a member of the Los Angeles Lakers in the 1980s.

Bob is originated from United States. He was teammates with another Hall of Famer, Magic Johnson, on the Los Angeles Lakers.
