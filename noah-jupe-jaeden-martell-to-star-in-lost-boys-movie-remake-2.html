Noah Jupe &amp; Jaeden Martell to Star in Lost Boys Movie Remake

Publish date: 2024-05-29

A new take on the classic movie The Lost Boys is in the works with some exciting young stars attached!

Noah Jupe and Jaeden Martell have been cast in the upcoming reimagined take on the 1980s teen vampire movie.

THR notes that the original film told the story of “two brothers who move to a California beach town that, the pair soon discovers, is being terrorized by a stylish vampire biker gang. Also taking bites from scenes were a pair of comic book-loving brothers who thought themselves vampire hunters.”

I Am Not Okay With This co-creator Jonathan Entwistle will be directing the upcoming movie with rising writer Randy McKinnon set to write the script.

Noah is best known for starring in the A Quiet Place movies and Honey Boy. Jaeden is best known for movies like Knives Out and It, as well as the Apple TV+ series Defending Jacob.
