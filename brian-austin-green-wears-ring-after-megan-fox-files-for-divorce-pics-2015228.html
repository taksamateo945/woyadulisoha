Publish date: 2024-06-07

The 42-year-old actor’s gold wedding band was clearly visible as he made a trip to a gas station. Green, who was wearing a white T-shirt, black shorts, red Nike sneakers, and sunglasses looked thin as he pumped gas into his white BMW.
