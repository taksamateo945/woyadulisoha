Austin Butler in Popular TV Shows

Austin Butler is a familiar face to TV audiences, having appeared in a number of popular shows throughout his career.

Butler’s breakout role on television came in 2009 when he was cast as the lead, Wilke, in the ABC Family drama series, Switched at Birth. He played the role for three seasons, earning critical acclaim for his performance as a teenage musician struggling with drug addiction.

Following his success on Switched at Birth, Butler landed a recurring role on The CW series, The Carrie Diaries. He played Sebastian Kydd, the love interest of the main character, for two seasons.

Most recently, Butler starred in the critically acclaimed drama series, The Shannara Chronicles, based on the books by Terry Brooks. He played the lead role of “Wil Ohmsford” for two seasons.
